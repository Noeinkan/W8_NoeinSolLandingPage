FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can you deliver a BEP?

Pre-appointment BEPs are typically delivered in 5–7 working days from receipt of the EIR and project information. Post-appointment BEPs take slightly longer due to the additional sections required (TIDP/MIDP, detailed execution plans). Complex or multi-volume EIRs may need an extended timeline — confirmed at scoping.

Do you work with firms outside the UK?

Yes. All engagements are delivered remotely. Clients are typically based in the UK, but ISO 19650 work is relevant globally and I’ve supported teams across Europe and the Middle East. Time zones are manageable with clear briefing and async communication.

What’s the difference between a pre- and post-appointment BEP?

A pre-appointment BEP is submitted at tender stage — it demonstrates your capability and approach before the contract is awarded. A post-appointment BEP builds on this: it’s developed after contract award with the full project team, confirming the detailed execution plan, TIDP/MIDP, and supply chain responsibilities.

Can you review a BEP we’ve already written?

Yes — BEP review and gap analysis is available as a standalone engagement. I assess your document against the EIR requirements and ISO 19650-2 and provide a structured recommendations report. This is priced separately; get in touch with your EIR and current draft for a quote.

Do you offer retained advisory or ongoing support?

Yes. For programmes that need ongoing IM oversight or periodic review, a retained arrangement can be structured — typically a set number of days per month at the day rate. This works well for clients who need consistent advisory without a full-time hire.

What’s Capsar.io and how does it relate to these services?

Capsar.io is a technology tool I built to read EIRs and assist in drafting BEP responses — it’s what makes fast, high-quality BEP production possible. It’s used in-house on BEP & EIR production engagements, and is also available as a standalone product for firms who want to produce their own BEPs faster.
