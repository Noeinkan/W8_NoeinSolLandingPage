Get your information management right — from the start

Firms know they need to manage information properly but don’t know where to start. The CDE exists but nobody uses it properly. Information requirements are copy-pasted from the last project and don’t reflect what’s actually needed. La soluzione

Andrea designs the information management framework end-to-end — CDE workflows, naming conventions, data governance, reporting structures, supply chain information requirements, and capability assessment processes. Strategy, setup, and adoption support.

Risultato

A system that people actually use — not a document that sits in a folder. Clear processes, consistent naming, functional reporting, and a supply chain that knows what to deliver across complex live programmes.

FAQ

Domande frequenti

How quickly can you deliver a BEP?

Pre-appointment BEPs are typically delivered in 5–7 working days from receipt of the EIR and project information. Post-appointment BEPs take slightly longer due to the additional sections required (TIDP/MIDP, detailed execution plans). Complex or multi-volume EIRs may need an extended timeline — confirmed at scoping.

Do you work con firms outside the UK?

Yes. All engagements are delivered remotely. Clients are typically based in the UK, but ISO 19650 work is relevant globally and I’ve supported teams across Europe and the Middle East. Time zones are manageable con clear briefing and async communication.

What’s the difference between a pre- and post-appointment BEP?

A pre-appointment BEP is submitted at tender stage — it demonstrates your capability and approach before the contract is awarded. A post-appointment BEP builds on this: it’s developed after contract award con the full project team, confirming the detailed execution plan, TIDP/MIDP, and supply chain responsibilities.

Can you review a BEP we’ve already written?

Yes — BEP review and gap analysis is available as a standalone engagement. I assess your document against the EIR requirements and ISO 19650-2 and provide a structured recommendations report. This is priced separately; get in touch con your EIR and current draft per un quote.

Do you offer retained advisory or ongoing support?

Yes. For programmes that need ongoing IM oversight or periodic review, a retained arrangement can be structured — typically a set number of days per month at the day rate. This works well for clients who need consistent advisory without a full-time hire.

What’s Capsar.io and how does it relate to these services?

Capsar.io is a technology tool I built to read EIRs and assist in drafting BEP responses — it’s what makes fast, high-quality BEP production possible. It’s used in-house on BEP & EIR production engagements, and is also available as a standalone product for firms who want to produce their own BEPs faster.

Not sure which service fits?

Describe what you’re working on and I’ll tell you which engagement makes sense — or whether a bespoke scope is the right approach.
