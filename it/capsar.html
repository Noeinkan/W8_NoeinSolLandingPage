Il problema

Perche i BEP sono ancora fragili

Quattro criticita che ogni BIM Manager e Information Manager riconosce subito.

01

Paralisi da pagina bianca

Every BEP starts from a blank template or a copy of the last one. There’s no structured starting point, so teams spend days just getting the document framework right before writing any actual content.

02

EIR complessi e poco strutturati

Client requirements are buried in 30–50 page documents con no structure. Extracting what matters — and tracking whether the BEP actually responds to it — is manual and error-prone.

03

Niente AI adatta a progetti riservati

Generic AI tools send your project data to third-party providers. On sensitive infrastructure and defence projects, that’s a non-starter. Teams need AI assistance without the privacy risk.

04

Competenza ISO 19650 presunta, non incorporata

Templates assume the author already knows the standard inside out. They don’t explain what each section needs, what good looks like, or how to align con the specific EIR. Expertise should be in the tool, not assumed from the user.
